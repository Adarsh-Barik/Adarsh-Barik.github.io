कहानियाँ / Stories

“मन कस्तूरी रे, जग दस्तूरी रे, बात हुई ना पूरी रे...”
- Varun Grover, Masaan (2015)
T
"The heart, like a wandering deer in search of its own fragrance, looks outward for what quietly resides within. In this customary world, our meetings are never complete..."
The literal translation doesn't do it justice. Here is an interpretation I like.

Everyone has a story to tell, yet very few of us pause long enough to truly listen. In my professional role, I interact with many people, especially students, but our conversations often remain confined to technical discussions. This initiative is my small attempt to step beyond that boundary: to meet new people, to exchange stories, and to quietly learn from one another.

I appreciate the inherently social nature of humanity, but, personally, social interactions are far from my comfort-zone. To make this experience comfortable and meaningful, I propose a few simple ground rules:

  • This is only open to people@IIT Delhi, keeping things logistically simple and manageable.
  • The format is open-close: all questions are open, yet you are free to respond only to what you are comfortable sharing.
  • You are free to choose your own stories to share. They need not even have a conclusion - closure, after all, is somewhat overrated. I am there to listen, not to advise, evaluate, or resolve.
  • And yes - the chai is on me.

If this resonates with you, let us meet.
